BMI Categories (WHO)

  • Underweight: BMI less than 18.5
  • Normal weight: BMI 18.5 to 24.9
  • Overweight: BMI 25 to 29.9
  • Obese: BMI 30 or greater

Note: BMI is a screening tool and does not directly measure body fat. Athletes and very muscular individuals may have a high BMI but low body fat.

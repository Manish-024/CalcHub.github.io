About CalcHub

CalcHub is a lightweight collection of practical calculators built to help students, professionals, and everyday users solve common numerical problems quickly and reliably. The project bundles calculators for finance, health, construction, education, internet speed, and more — all in a fast, privacy-friendly static site.

Core principles

  • Privacy-first: no user data collection unless explicitly stated.
  • Accessible: readable typography and keyboard-friendly controls.
  • Reliable: accurate formulas and clear results with helpful context.
  • Open-source: licensed under the MIT license and available on GitHub.

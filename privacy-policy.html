Privacy Policy

Last updated: Nov 28, 2025

Overview

CalcHub is a static site offering client-side calculators. We prioritize your privacy and do not collect personal data by default. Most calculators run entirely in your browser; results are not transmitted to our servers.

What we collect

  • No personal data is collected unless you voluntarily contact us (for example via the email address provided).
  • We may use analytics or error reporting on some deployments; where used, it will be disclosed and limited to non-identifying data. Check the deployed site footer or privacy link for specific disclosures.

Third-party services

External links (GitHub, CDN-hosted assets) are used to deliver fonts and icons. These services may collect anonymized usage data — please review their policies if concerned.

Cookies

This site does not use cookies for personalization. If cookies are introduced in future versions they will be disclosed here.
